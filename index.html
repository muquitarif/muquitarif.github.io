01. PROFILE_OVERVIEW

Engineering Intelligent Hardware for a Smarter World.

I am a Hardware Design Engineer with a passion for turning complex problems into elegant, manufacturable PCB assemblies. My work focuses on the intersection of high-speed digital design and low-power embedded systems.

I specialize in creating high-performance PCB assemblies with a focus on signal integrity and power distribution. My passion for problem-solving drives me to continuously learn and adapt to new challenges in the hardware space. I am committed to leveraging my technical expertise to build innovative solutions that have a meaningful, positive impact on people's lives worldwide.
